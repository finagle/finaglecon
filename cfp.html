Call for Papers

The CFP deadline is Tuesday, June 30th, 2015 at midnight (PST).

#FinagleCon is an annual conference organized by the Finagle community, bringing together users and developers to share and learn about the project and its growing ecosystem. The conference will feature one day of talks and tutorials by adopters and contributors about Finagle, Finatra, Finch, and related technologies. To help encourage the open collaboration and discussions that are necessary to help the greater Finagle ecosystem be successful, we invite Finagle adopters and contributors to submit a proposal for the event.
